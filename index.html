SONEC’s Role

What SONEC Does

SONEC is envisioned as a system level institution supporting more coordinated, transparent, and better planned electricity development in Somalia.

System Operation

Support operational visibility across the sector and strengthen understanding of system conditions, capacity margins, and reliability performance.

Strategic Energy Planning

Provide structured insight to support future network development, investment decisions, and long term planning for Somalia’s power sector.

Sector Coordination

Encourage a more connected and coordinated approach between utilities, infrastructure planning, and electricity system development.

Data and Reporting

Improve the use of operational data, reporting, and system indicators to support better visibility and more informed decision making.

Why It Matters

Building the Foundations for a More Integrated Power System

Somalia’s electricity sector is developing in a context where coordination, visibility, and long term planning are increasingly important. SONEC represents a structured approach to improving system understanding, supporting future integration, and creating a stronger basis for reliable and strategic energy development.

Better visibility Stronger understanding of current system conditions and trends.
Better planning Improved support for future operational and infrastructure decisions.
Better coordination A clearer framework for connecting system information and sector priorities.
