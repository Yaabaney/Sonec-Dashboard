ABOUT SONEC

Supporting Coordination, Visibility, and Strategic Energy Planning in Somalia

SONEC is envisioned as a system level institution supporting better operational visibility, improved coordination, and more structured strategic planning across Somalia’s electricity sector.

INSTITUTIONAL OVERVIEW

What SONEC Represents

SONEC is intended to support the development of a more coordinated and informed electricity sector in Somalia. Its role is to contribute to greater system visibility, stronger planning foundations, and improved understanding of operational conditions across the sector.

Operational Visibility

Supporting a clearer view of system conditions, demand patterns, outages, available capacity, and key operational indicators.

Planning Support

Contributing structured information and analysis to support long term energy planning, infrastructure priorities, and future system development.

Sector Coordination

Encouraging a more connected and coordinated approach between utilities, future network development, and energy system decision making.

Strategic Development

Helping build the institutional and analytical foundations needed for a more integrated, transparent, and reliable power system over time.

TECHNICAL ADVISOR

Technical Advisory and Sector Development Support

SONEC is supported by technical advisory input focused on power systems, strategic planning, system visibility, and the long term development of Somalia’s electricity sector.

This advisory role is intended to help shape practical tools, structured reporting, and system level thinking that can support a more coordinated and data informed energy future.

Bashir Hussain

Technical Advisor

Bashir Hussain is an electrical engineer focused on power systems, strategic planning, and sector development. His work supports SONEC’s vision through system visibility, coordinated planning, and practical digital tools for energy decision making.

DEVELOPMENT FOCUS

What This Platform Is Building

The SONEC website is being developed as a practical platform to improve sector visibility and provide a structured public facing foundation for future system operation and strategic planning work.

Dashboard and Visibility

A central dashboard for presenting energy indicators, utility level information, maps, operational summaries, and performance trends.

Reporting and Publications

A dedicated reports section for publishing PDF reports, planning notes, sector summaries, and future technical publications.

Planning Foundation

A growing platform that can support future data collection, power system studies, network planning, and broader institutional development.
